# CentricVista
CentricVista Website - Full Development Package

Included:
- index.html
- css/style.css
- js/script.js
- assets/images/centricvista-logo.png
- assets/images/centricvista-icon.png

Sections included:
- Home / Hero
- Services
- VA Packages
- AI Services
- Power BI Dashboard section
- Pricing
- Booking calendar placeholder
- Testimonials
- About
- Recruiting form
- FAQ
- Contact form
- Scroll animations

Important:
The forms and booking calendar are front-end placeholders only. They show a success message but do not send data anywhere yet.
To make them real, connect the forms to Formspree, Netlify Forms, EmailJS, a CRM, or a backend server.
Replace https://calendly.com/ with your real Calendly or Google booking link.
Replace info@centricvista.com and +1 (555) 000-0000 with your real business contact details.

How to open:
1. Unzip the folder.
2. Open index.html in your browser.
3. Keep the folders exactly as they are so the CSS, JS, and images load correctly.
